About Coat & Finish

Chicago’s trusted painters — delivering quality craftsmanship, honest service, and beautiful results since day one.

Our Story

Coat & Finish was founded with a simple mission: provide Chicago homeowners and businesses with painting services they can truly trust. We saw too many corners being cut in the industry — rushed prep, cheap materials, unreliable crews — and we knew there had to be a better way.

Every project we take on receives the same level of care, whether it’s a single room refresh or a full exterior repaint. We believe that preparation is the foundation of every great paint job, that premium materials are worth the investment, and that our clients deserve clear communication and respect throughout the process.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Meticulous preparation — We spend as much time prepping as painting, because that’s what separates good work from great work.
  • Premium materials — We use top-quality paints from Benjamin Moore, Sherwin-Williams, and other trusted brands.
  • Skilled craftsmen — Our painters are experienced professionals, not day laborers. They take pride in precision.
  • Clear communication — You’ll always know what’s happening, when, and why. No surprises.
  • Satisfaction guarantee — We’re not done until you’re thrilled with the results.
