Painting Services in the West Loop, Chicago

The West Loop is one of Chicago's fastest-growing neighborhoods, known for its converted lofts, luxury condos, world-class restaurants, and thriving commercial district. Coat & Finish provides painting services designed for the unique properties and fast-paced lifestyle of the West Loop.

West Loop Painting Services

  • Loft and industrial conversion painting
  • Restaurant and hospitality painting

  • Retail storefront interior and exterior painting
  • Building common area maintenance

Condo and Loft Painting

West Loop condos range from sleek new high-rises to converted warehouse lofts with exposed brick, timber beams, and soaring ceilings. We have the equipment and expertise to handle high ceilings, large open floor plans, and the mix of industrial and modern elements that define West Loop living.

Commercial Painting for West Loop Businesses

The West Loop's vibrant restaurant row and growing office market keep our commercial team busy. We paint restaurants, bars, retail spaces, galleries, and offices with flexible scheduling that works around your business hours. Fast turnarounds and premium finishes are our standard.
