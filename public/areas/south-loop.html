Painting in the South Loop, Chicago

The South Loop has transformed from an industrial corridor into a thriving residential neighborhood with modern condos, townhomes, and a growing commercial district. Coat & Finish provides professional painting services for the South Loop's diverse property types, from high-rise condos with skyline views to street-level townhomes and retail spaces.

South Loop Services

  • High-rise and mid-rise condo painting
  • Townhome interior and exterior painting
  • New construction finish painting
  • Commercial and retail painting
  • Property management turnover painting
  • Drywall repair and touch-up services

High-Rise Condo Expertise

The South Loop is home to many of Chicago's modern high-rise buildings. We're experienced with the logistics of painting in high-rise condos — elevator reservations, freight access, building rules, and the coordination required to move materials in and out efficiently.

South Loop Property Management

With a large rental market in the South Loop, we work closely with property managers to provide reliable, consistent painting for unit turnovers, common area maintenance, and building-wide refresh projects. We offer competitive rates for ongoing partnerships.
