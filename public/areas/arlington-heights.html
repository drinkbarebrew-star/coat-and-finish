Arlington Heights Home Styles

The village features a variety of home styles including mid-century ranches, split-levels, colonials, and newer construction. Each style has its own painting considerations — from the long, low rooflines of ranches to the multi-story facades of colonials. We adapt our approach to deliver the best results for your specific home.
