Painting Services in Logan Square

Logan Square is a dynamic Chicago neighborhood blending historic charm with modern energy. From iconic Chicago bungalows and brick two-flats along the boulevard system to new condos and renovated greystones, Logan Square properties deserve painting services that honor their character while delivering modern quality.

What We Paint in Logan Square

  • Chicago bungalow interiors and exteriors
  • Multi-unit building common areas and individual units
  • Historic greystone restoration and painting
  • Modern condo and loft painting
  • Commercial spaces along Milwaukee Avenue
  • Deck, porch, and garage painting

Chicago Bungalow Specialists

Logan Square is home to hundreds of classic Chicago bungalows, and we love painting them. We understand the craftsmanship in these homes — the decorative plaster, wood trim, built-in cabinetry, and unique architectural details that deserve careful, expert painting. We preserve and highlight these features rather than painting over them.

Growing with Logan Square

As Logan Square continues to evolve, we're here for every type of project — from restoring a vintage two-flat to finishing a brand-new mixed-use development. Our painters are proud to be part of this neighborhood's growth.
