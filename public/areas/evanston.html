House Painting in Evanston, IL

Evanston combines the charm of a college town with the sophistication of a North Shore suburb. Home to Northwestern University and a diverse range of housing from Victorian mansions to mid-century ranches, Evanston properties deserve painting services that match the community's high standards. Coat & Finish delivers professional painting throughout Evanston.

From grand lakefront estates on Sheridan Road to charming bungalows in South Evanston, the city offers remarkable architectural variety. Our painters are experienced with Victorian, Craftsman, Colonial Revival, Tudor, and contemporary homes throughout Evanston's distinct neighborhoods.
