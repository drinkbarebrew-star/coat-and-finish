Painting Services in Hyde Park, Chicago

Hyde Park is a neighborhood rich in history and architectural significance, home to the University of Chicago, the Museum of Science and Industry, and some of the most impressive residential architecture on Chicago's South Side. Coat & Finish provides painting services that respect and enhance Hyde Park's unique character.

Hyde Park Painting Services

  • Historic home painting and color restoration
  • Interior painting for homes, apartments, and condos
  • Exterior painting for single-family and multi-unit properties
  • University-area rental property painting
  • Cabinet and kitchen painting
  • Plaster repair and restoration

Historic Architecture in Hyde Park

Hyde Park's housing stock includes Prairie-style homes, grand mansions, Art Deco apartments, and mid-century buildings with significant architectural merit. Our painters understand how to work with the materials and design elements of each era — from Frank Lloyd Wright-influenced woodwork to ornate plaster moldings and leaded glass surrounds.

University Area Services

The University of Chicago campus and surrounding area generate steady demand for quality painting services. We work with homeowners, landlords, and the university community to maintain the neighborhood's high standards of property care.
