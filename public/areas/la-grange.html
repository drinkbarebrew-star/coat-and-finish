Painting Services in La Grange, IL

La Grange is a gem of the western suburbs — a walkable, community-oriented village with beautiful homes, a vibrant downtown, and deep roots. Coat & Finish provides professional painting services for La Grange homeowners who value quality craftsmanship and personal service.

La Grange Painting Services

  • Interior and exterior residential painting
  • Historic home painting and color restoration
  • Cabinet painting and kitchen refreshes
  • Deck, porch, and railing staining
  • Wallpaper removal and surface preparation
  • Commercial painting for La Grange businesses

La Grange's Architectural Charm

La Grange's housing stock includes beautiful Victorians, Craftsman bungalows, Prairie-style homes, and classic American four-squares. Many homes date to the early 1900s and feature original woodwork, leaded glass, and architectural details that deserve careful, expert painting. We love preserving and showcasing these details.
