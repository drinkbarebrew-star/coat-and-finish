Exterior Painting in Chicago, IL

Durable, beautiful exterior painting that protects your Chicago home against harsh Midwest weather.

Chicago Exterior Painting Experts

Chicago weather is tough on homes. From freezing winters and ice storms to hot, humid summers, your home's exterior takes a beating year-round. Coat & Finish provides exterior painting that not only looks beautiful but also protects your investment for years to come.

We paint siding, trim, fascia, soffits, shutters, porches, decks, and more. Whether your home is clad in wood, fiber cement, vinyl, stucco, or brick, we have the experience and materials to deliver a flawless, long-lasting finish.

Our Exterior Painting Process

  • Comprehensive inspection — We assess every surface for peeling, cracking, rot, mildew, and other issues that need attention before painting.
  • Power washing — We thoroughly clean all surfaces to remove dirt, mildew, chalking paint, and debris for optimal adhesion.
  • Repair and prep — We scrape loose paint, fill cracks, replace rotted wood, caulk gaps, and prime bare surfaces.
  • Premium application — We apply two coats of weather-resistant, UV-protective paint using brushes, rollers, and airless sprayers as appropriate.
  • Cleanup and inspection — We remove all equipment, clean up your property, and do a final walkthrough together.

Weather-Resistant Coatings

We use exterior-grade paints formulated for Midwest conditions. Our products resist fading, cracking, peeling, and mildew growth. We match the right coating to your surface material for maximum durability — typically 7-10 years between repaints.

Boost Your Curb Appeal

A fresh exterior paint job is one of the highest-ROI home improvements you can make. Whether you're keeping your current color scheme or making a dramatic change, we help you choose colors that complement your architecture and neighborhood.
