Expert Interior Painting in Chicago

Your home's interior is where life happens. At Coat & Finish, we treat every interior painting project with the care and precision it deserves. From single accent walls to whole-home repaints, our Chicago painters deliver stunning results that elevate your living spaces.

We work with homeowners across Chicago and the surrounding suburbs to bring their vision to life. Whether you're refreshing a nursery, modernizing a dated dining room, or preparing a property for sale, our interior painting services are designed to exceed expectations.

Our Interior Painting Process

  • Color consultation — We help you choose the perfect palette for your space, considering lighting, furnishings, and personal style.
  • Surface preparation — Thorough cleaning, sanding, patching, and priming ensure a smooth, long-lasting finish.
  • Premium materials — We use top-tier paints from Benjamin Moore, Sherwin-Williams, and other trusted brands.
  • Clean workmanship — Drop cloths, painter's tape, and meticulous edge work protect your home and deliver crisp results.
  • Final walkthrough — We inspect every surface with you to ensure complete satisfaction before we leave.

Rooms We Paint

No room is too big or too small. We paint living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, bathrooms, hallways, staircases, basements, and home offices. We also handle specialty finishes like accent walls, two-tone color schemes, and textured effects.

Why Chicago Homeowners Choose Us

Chicago homes come in all shapes and eras — from vintage Victorians in Lincoln Park to modern condos in the West Loop. Our painters understand the nuances of different wall surfaces, plaster conditions, and architectural details. We adapt our approach to deliver perfect results every time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Most single-room projects take 1-2 days, while whole-home repaints typically take 3-7 days depending on the size and scope. We provide a detailed timeline during your free estimate so you can plan accordingly.
We handle all furniture moving and protection. We carefully shift furniture to the center of each room and cover everything with drop cloths. For large or fragile items, we recommend moving them to another room before we arrive.
We use premium paints from Benjamin Moore, Sherwin-Williams, and Behr. We recommend specific sheens for each room — flat or matte for ceilings, eggshell or satin for walls, and semi-gloss for trim, doors, and high-moisture areas like bathrooms.
