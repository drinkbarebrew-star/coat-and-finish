Epoxy Flooring in Chicago

Epoxy flooring transforms dull, stained concrete into a beautiful, durable surface that resists chemicals, abrasion, and moisture. At Coat & Finish, we install premium epoxy coatings for residential garages, basements, workshops, and commercial spaces throughout Chicago and the suburbs.

Our Epoxy Flooring Process

  • Concrete preparation — We diamond-grind or shot-blast the concrete surface to create an ideal profile for epoxy adhesion. This is the most critical step for a long-lasting floor.
  • Crack and joint repair — We fill cracks, divots, and spalled areas with epoxy filler for a smooth, even base.
  • Primer coat — A penetrating epoxy primer seals the concrete and bonds to the surface.
  • Base coat — We apply the main epoxy color coat in your chosen color and style.
  • Decorative flakes — Optional vinyl flake chips broadcast into the wet epoxy create a granite-like appearance and add texture.
  • Clear top coat — A high-build clear polyaspartic or polyurethane top coat provides UV stability, chemical resistance, and a glossy or satin finish.

Epoxy Flooring Options

We offer solid color epoxy, metallic epoxy (swirled, marble-like effects), flake chip systems, and quartz broadcast systems. Color options are virtually unlimited. We bring samples to your consultation so you can see and feel the actual finished product.

Garage Floor Coatings

Our most popular residential application is garage floor epoxy. A coated garage floor resists hot tire marks, road salt, oil drips, and chemical spills. It's easy to clean, looks incredible, and adds value to your home. Most residential garage floors are completed in 2-3 days.
