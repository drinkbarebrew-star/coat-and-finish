Drywall Repair Services in Chicago

Drywall damage is inevitable in any home. From doorknob holes and nail pops to water damage and settling cracks, imperfections accumulate over time. Coat & Finish provides professional drywall repair that restores your walls and ceilings to a smooth, seamless condition.

What We Repair

  • Small holes and dents — Nail holes, screw pops, doorknob dings, and minor impact damage.
  • Medium holes — Fist-sized holes from accidents, plumbing access, or removed fixtures.
  • Large holes — Major damage requiring new drywall sections to be cut, fitted, and taped.
  • Cracks — Hairline cracks, corner cracks, and settling cracks in walls and ceilings.
  • Water damage — Stained, bubbled, or softened drywall from leaks, flooding, or condensation.
  • Texture matching — We match existing wall textures including smooth, orange peel, knockdown, and popcorn ceiling textures.

Our Repair Process

Every drywall repair follows the same fundamental steps: assess the damage, remove any compromised material, install new drywall or patching compound, tape and mud the joints, sand smooth, and prime for painting. The difference is in the execution — our experienced finishers produce invisible repairs that blend seamlessly with your existing walls.

When to Call a Professional

While small nail holes are easy DIY fixes, anything larger than a few inches benefits from professional repair. Improper patching often results in visible bumps, ridges, and texture mismatches that are obvious under paint. Our repairs are invisible — you won't be able to tell where the damage was.
