Commercial Painting in Chicago

Your commercial space makes a statement about your business. Whether you're refreshing an office, rebranding a retail store, or maintaining a multi-unit property, Coat & Finish delivers professional commercial painting that enhances your image and protects your property.

We work with businesses throughout Chicago and the suburbs, providing flexible scheduling — including nights and weekends — to minimize disruption to your operations.

Commercial Services

  • Office spaces — Cubicle areas, private offices, conference rooms, lobbies, and common areas.
  • Retail stores — Storefronts, showrooms, fitting rooms, and back-of-house areas.
  • Restaurants and hospitality — Dining areas, kitchens, bars, and hotel rooms with food-safe, low-odor products.
  • Medical and dental offices — Clean, sanitary finishes that meet healthcare facility standards.
  • Property management — Turnover painting, hallways, lobbies, and common areas for apartments and condos.
  • Industrial facilities — Warehouses, factories, safety markings, and specialty coatings.

Why Businesses Choose Coat & Finish

We understand that commercial projects have different priorities than residential work. You need reliable scheduling, minimal disruption, competitive pricing for larger scopes, and the ability to work around your business hours. Our commercial team is experienced in managing these demands while delivering high-quality results.

Flexible Scheduling

We offer after-hours, overnight, and weekend painting to keep your business running during the project. Our crews are efficient, professional, and respectful of your workspace and customers.
