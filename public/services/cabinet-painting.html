Kitchen Cabinet Painting in Chicago

A full kitchen renovation can cost tens of thousands of dollars. Cabinet painting offers a dramatic transformation at a fraction of the price. At Coat & Finish, we specialize in turning dated, worn cabinets into beautiful, modern focal points that completely change the look of your kitchen.

Our cabinet painting process goes far beyond a simple brush-and-roll job. We remove doors and hardware, thoroughly clean and sand every surface, apply primer and multiple coats of durable cabinet-grade paint, and reassemble everything with precision.

Our Cabinet Refinishing Process

  • Door and hardware removal — Every door, drawer front, and piece of hardware is carefully labeled and removed for off-site or on-site finishing.
  • Deep cleaning and degreasing — Kitchen cabinets accumulate years of grease and grime. We thoroughly clean every surface with professional-grade degreasers.
  • Sanding and priming — We sand all surfaces for adhesion and apply bonding primer designed specifically for cabinetry.
  • Paint application — We apply 2-3 coats of high-quality cabinet enamel, sanding lightly between coats for a glass-smooth finish.
  • Reassembly — We reinstall all doors with new or existing hardware, adjust hinges, and ensure perfect alignment.

Popular Cabinet Colors

White and off-white remain the most popular choices, but we're seeing strong demand for navy blue, sage green, charcoal gray, and two-tone combinations. We offer unlimited color options and can match any shade you love.

Cabinet Painting vs. Replacement

Cabinet painting typically costs 50-75% less than full cabinet replacement while achieving a similar visual impact. If your existing cabinets are structurally sound, painting is the smart choice for a kitchen refresh.
