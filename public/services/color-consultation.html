Professional Color Consultation in Chicago

Choosing the right paint color can feel overwhelming. With thousands of options and complex interactions between lighting, furnishings, and architecture, it's easy to second-guess your choices. Coat & Finish offers professional color consultation to help you make confident decisions you'll love for years.

What's Included

  • In-home assessment — We evaluate your space's natural and artificial lighting, existing finishes, furniture, and architectural features.
  • Curated palette — We present a refined selection of colors tailored to your style, budget, and the character of your home.
  • Large-format samples — We apply test swatches directly to your walls so you can see exactly how colors look in your actual space.
  • Full-home flow — For multi-room projects, we ensure colors transition harmoniously from room to room.
  • Exterior coordination — We consider roofing, stonework, landscaping, and neighborhood context for exterior color selections.

Why Color Consultation Matters

Paint looks completely different on a small chip versus a full wall. Lighting changes throughout the day. Colors interact with flooring, cabinetry, and textiles in unexpected ways. Our consultants understand these dynamics and save you from costly color mistakes.

Popular Color Trends in Chicago

We're seeing strong demand for warm whites, earthy greens, and deep navy blues in Chicago interiors. For exteriors, classic combinations like charcoal with white trim and deep green with cream accents remain popular, especially on Chicago's historic homes.
