Pressure Washing in Chicago, IL

Restore the beauty of your Chicago property with professional pressure washing and soft washing.

Pressure Washing Services in Chicago

Over time, Chicago's weather deposits dirt, algae, mildew, pollution, and grime on every outdoor surface. Pressure washing instantly restores the appearance of your home or business, removing years of buildup and revealing the clean surface underneath. Coat & Finish provides professional pressure washing for residential and commercial properties throughout Chicago.

What We Clean

  • House siding — Vinyl, wood, fiber cement, stucco, and brick. We adjust pressure levels to each material.
  • Driveways and walkways — Concrete, pavers, asphalt, and stone surfaces.
  • Patios and pool decks — Restore slip-resistant surfaces and remove green algae buildup.
  • Fences — Wood, vinyl, and composite fencing brought back to life.
  • Decks — Pre-stain prep or standalone cleaning to remove gray weathering and mildew.
  • Roofs and gutters — Soft wash methods for safe, effective cleaning without damage.

Pressure Washing vs. Soft Washing

Not all surfaces can handle high-pressure cleaning. We use soft washing — a low-pressure method with specialized cleaning solutions — for delicate surfaces like roofing, painted siding, and older wood. For hard surfaces like concrete and brick, we use higher pressures for deep cleaning.

Pre-Paint Prep

Pressure washing is an essential first step before any exterior painting project. It removes all contaminants that can prevent paint adhesion. When you hire us for exterior painting, thorough pressure washing is always included in our prep process.
